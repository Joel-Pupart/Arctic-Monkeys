MEMBERS

Alex Turner

Lead vocals, keyboards, synthesizers and piano. He is the frontman and the principal songwriter of the Arctic Monkeys. He has contributed to six albums with the band.

Jamie Cook

Lead and rhythm guitar, keyboards and backing vocals. He is cosidered the bands "indie music fanatic" and supposedly turned the band to what it is today.

Matt Helders

Drums, backing and lead vocals. One of the founding members of the band. The most comprehensive backing vocalist of the group and sings lead vocals in "D is for Dangerous".

Nicholas O'Malley

Bass, backing vocals. O'Malley was drafted in as a temporary replacement for bassist Andy Nicholson. Nicholson's departure from the band saw the announcement that O'Malley will become a full member of the band.
